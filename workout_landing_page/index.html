The Concept

BRO MODE
UNLOCKED.

Every single exercise in our programmes is designed to be done with another person. Not just a spotter — a training partner who pushes you, competes against you, and makes it impossible to phone it in.

Think push-ups where you high-five mid-rep. Balance challenges where you need each other to stay upright. Strength drills where one of you is the resistance. It's training reimagined from the ground up.

✓ Nobody sits while the other works — you're both moving, always
✓ Built-in accountability — your bro is waiting for you
✓ Competitive elements that make you push harder without even noticing
✓ Fresh workouts every time — no two sessions feel the same

The Team

WHO WE ARE.

Two guys who got tired of the same thing and decided to fix it.

D
Former Pro Athlete

DAN

I've trained at the highest level — where every session has a purpose, a structure, and a reason to show up. When I moved away from professional sport, I couldn't find a single programme that gave me that same buzz. Gym was just... repetitive. So I started building something different, using everything I learned about what makes athletes keep coming back and getting better. That became GymBros.

D
Gym Enthusiast & Co-Founder

DUMI

I'm not a pro athlete. I'm just someone who genuinely loves going to the gym and feeling healthy — but hated that it always felt like a chore after a few months. I needed variety, competition, and someone to suffer with. When Dan showed me what training for two could look like, everything changed. I'm living proof that this works for regular people who just want to enjoy the process.
