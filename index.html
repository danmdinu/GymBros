The Concept

BRO MODE
UNLOCKED.

Every single exercise in our programmes is designed to be done with another person. Not just a spotter but a training partner who pushes you, competes against you, and makes it impossible to phone it in. That means you and someone you already know — friend, teammate, flatmate. Not an app that pairs you with a stranger. Bro or sistah, same workouts. We're just here to make them good.

Research shows people stick to training better when they're not doing it alone. A training partner means more sessions, more effort, and way more fun.

Think push-ups where you high-five mid-rep. Balance challenges where you need each other to stay upright. Strength drills where one of you is the resistance. Training reimagined from the ground up for two people who actually want to be there.

We raided football, basketball, rugby and athletics for the movement patterns that actually build athletes and rebuilt them for the gym floor. This is what team sport training looks like when two regular people do it together.

✓ Nobody sits while the other works. You're both moving, always.
✓ Built-in accountability. Your bro is waiting for you.
✓ Competitive elements that make you push harder without even noticing.
✓ Fresh workouts every time. No two sessions feel the same.
